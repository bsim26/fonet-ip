About Fonet-IP

We are a licensed low voltage contractor founded in 1998, has grown to serve a 15-county area in central Florida. We provide complete solutions for any business telephone communication requirement, CCTV systems and structured wiring. Our quality team of industry certified technicians ensures our clients receive a superior level of service.

A Samsung dealer since 2001, Fonet-IP offers high quality products and expert service to businesses of all sizes. Fonet-IP features the Samsung Officeserv 7000 and Samsung SCM IP phone systems which enable a variety of configurations for any business, including multi-site and remote environments. Scalability and convergence of device types assures a system that can grow with your business. Fonet-IP also services most major brands of phone systems.
